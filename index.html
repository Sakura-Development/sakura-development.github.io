Mugen

A blockchain by Sakura Development

About Mugen

Mugen is a blockchain developed in JavaScript.

Mugen aims to further decentralize cryptocurrency by taking power away from the mining warehouses. Mugen uses a CPU mining algorithm to prevent graphics card farms from competing with the common consumer for cards and the average miner for crypto.

Native Crypto

Mugen Coin (MUGN) is a simple crypto based on the Mugen blockchain.

Mugen Coin is the Mugen blockchain's native coin. Other coins will be able to be created off of the Mugen chain in the future, however, MUGN is currently the only coin based on Mugen and it will most likely stay that way for quite some time.
